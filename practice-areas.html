Frequently Asked Questions
  • How does social media impact family law cases?
    Social media activity can be used as evidence in family law disputes, affecting divorce, custody, and other legal proceedings.
  • What legal protections exist for coerced marriages?
    Coerced marriages are illegal, and victims can seek legal intervention to annul the marriage and pursue legal protection.
  • What is a family visa, and who qualifies?
    A family visa allows close relatives of a resident or citizen to join them legally in the country, subject to eligibility requirements.
  • Do I need legal advice to start a business?
    Seeking legal advice ensures compliance with business laws, helps in contract drafting, and protects against potential legal risks.
  • What are the different types of business entities?
    Business entities include sole proprietorships, partnerships, corporations, and LLCs, each with unique legal implications.
