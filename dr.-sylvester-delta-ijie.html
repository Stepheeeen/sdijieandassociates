Strategic, scholarly, and client-focused – a legal leader with a passion for excellence.

DR. SYLVESTER DELTA IJIE is a Barrister and Solicitor of the Supreme Court of Nigeria and the Principal Partner of S. D. IJIE & ASSOCIATES, Legal Practitioners, and Notaries Public. He holds an LL.B (Hons) from Edo State University, Ekpoma, a B.L from the Nigerian Law School, an LL.M in Commercial Law from the University of Lagos, and a Ph.D in Law (Jurisprudence and International Law) from Ambrose Alli University, Ekpoma.

Dr. Ijie brings decades of experience across Maritime/Admiralty Law, Corporate Practice, Property Law, Intellectual Property, Corporate Finance, M&A, Debt Recovery, and both Civil and Criminal Litigation. His practice is driven by deep research, ethical advocacy, and unwavering commitment to justice.

With a reputation for delivering tailored legal solutions and strategic counsel, Dr. Ijie’s advisory role extends to legal consultancy and boardroom compliance. His work continues to shape the landscape of corporate and commercial law practice in Nigeria.
