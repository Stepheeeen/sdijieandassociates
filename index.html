Trusted Legal Advisors
Delivering Exceptional Legal Solutions

Dr. Sylvester Delta Ijie is a Barrister and Solicitor of the Supreme Court of Nigeria and the Principal Partner of S. D. IJIE & ASSOCIATES, Legal Practitioners, and Notaries Public. He obtained his Bachelor of Laws (LL.B Hons) from Edo State University, Ekpoma, and his B.L from the Nigerian Law School. He furthered his education with a Master’s in Law (LL.M) in Commercial Law from the University of Lagos, and a Doctorate (Ph.D) in Law specializing in Jurisprudence and International Law from Ambrose Alli University, Ekpoma. As a seasoned legal expert, Dr. Ijie has extensive experience in Maritime/Admiralty Law, Company Secretariat and Corporate Practices, Property Law, Intellectual Property, Corporate Finance, Mergers & Acquisitions, Debt Recovery, and both Civil & Criminal Litigation. His expertise extends to legal research and consultancy, making him a formidable advocate in Nigerian courts. Driven by a commitment to justice, he continues to provide strategic legal counsel, ensuring clients receive top-tier representation and legal solutions tailored to their needs.
